The Story of Asif Farms

Located in the Heart of Rahim Yar Khan

Asif Farms is a luxury agricultural estate specializing in premium mango cultivation Since 2009. Founded by Mr. Asif Jamal, it stands as a proud project of Asif and Sons.

Rooted in Rahim Yar Khan, a region globally celebrated for its fertile soil and perfect climate, our farm employs sustainable, state-of-the-art agricultural practices. We ensure that every single mango—from our signature Chaunsa to the classic Sindhri—meets stringent export quality benchmarks.

Our mission is uncompromising: To deliver the authentic taste of Pakistani summers, perfectly ripe, fragrant, and pristine, directly from our trees to your doorstep using modern automated delivery logic.
